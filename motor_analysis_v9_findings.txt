1. PRIMARY LIMIT: Temperature-adjusted encoder tick count

2. SECONDARY LIMIT: Fixed timeout after expected completion
   timeout = 1500 ms (1.5 seconds)

3. TERTIARY LIMIT: Absolute maximum timeout
